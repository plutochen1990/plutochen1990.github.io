Biography

Currently, I'm a post-doctoral fellow at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) and AI Chip Center for Emerging Smart Systems Limited (ACCESS), under the supervision of Prof. Bei Yu. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from CUHK in 2021. Previously, I received M.Eng. and B.Eng. in Electronics Engineering from Southeast University (SEU) in 2017 and 2014, respectively.

My research interests include machine learning in EDA, design space exploration, analog/mixed-signal VLSI design-for-reliability.
