About

I'm an Assistant Professor in the School of Science and Engineering, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. Previously, I was a post-doctoral fellow at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) and a post-doctoral researcher at the Chair of Electronic Design Automation, the Technical University of Munich (TUM). I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from CUHK advised by Prof. Bei Yu. My research interests include VLSI CAD and deep learning accelerators for edge devices.

I have openings for highly motivated M.Phil./Ph.D. students and Research Assistants with background in Electronic and Computer Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering or other related fields. Please contact me with your CV and transcript if you’re interested.
